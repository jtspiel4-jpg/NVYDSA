What is the Northview YDSA Club?


the Northview Young Democratic Socialists of America (NVYDSA) is a way for you to get involved and create change in our community. We will host educational activities, lead community efforts, and participate in local government. Fun activities, arts-n-crafts, democratic elections, and socialist led conversations will constitute club meetings. Meetings will be held on biweekly tuesdays in Mrs. Rauschers room.











What is the DSA?


The Democratic Socialists of America is the largest socialist organization in the United States, with over 80,000 members and chapters in all 50 states. We believe that working people should run both the economy and society democratically to meet human needs, not to make profits for a few.(DSA)

Can i earn volunteer hours by participating in events?


yes! we plan on eventually starting out own volunteer events which will be applicable for the Volunteer Hours Seal. Other activities may also further you in Government class activities such as the Government Engagement Journal assignments.
